About KnightStable

KnightStable is made for people trying to explore games and resources. With this site, you can find ranging from 100 all the way up the 2800s, you can use these games to analyze openings, style of play, learn from higher level games or see how people at your level are playing.

Aside from searching openings, you can also search for openings and see which one suits your play style. Are you an agressive player? Maybe take a look at the Dutch Defense or Kings Indian Defense. Are you a sharp player? The Tal Variation will suit you the best! We have the most famous as well as the most obsecure openings in our list so feel free to poke around!

We also recommend you check out the recommened section for awesome sites related to chess, some are for learning chess and some for playing and many more. These sites are used all around the world regularly by many.

Enjoy your time here at KnightStable!
