Recommended

Lichess - This is a free, non-profit website where people can enjoy and play with each other. They offer many things such as free puzzles, teaching videos and variants aswell. They have a huge playerbase and a responsive staff team.

Link - https://lichess.org/

Lichess Database - Most of the games here are from the Lichess Database, which has a collection of many games over the years of Lichess in a .pgn.zst file. The databases are seprated by month all the way from January 2013 to current time. In total they have just shy of 4 billion games.

Link - https://database.lichess.org/

Chess.com - Another awesome site to play chess on with others. Just like Lichess, Chess.com has puzzles, videos, tutorials ,variants and more. However, it has features behind pay walls but many have said that the subscriptions were worth the returns.

Link - https://www.chess.com/home

All of these are awesome places and we reccomend you check them out.
